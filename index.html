About GitteCharity (Nurtura)

GitteCharity is a charitable initiative dedicated to education, food, shelter, and animal welfare. We connect donors with people who need essentials — ensuring dignity through a simple, transparent and community-driven platform.

We are building a safe marketplace where anyone can give an item or claim an item for free. Owners may ask claimants to pay delivery charges only. Cash donations help us expand reach.
